Built with care

AI se banaya, dil se socha.

A companion app only works if it's actually safe to open. Here's what that means in practice.

Every face is AI-generated

No real person's photo is ever used for a companion. Every character you talk to is entirely synthetic, start to finish.

Your conversations stay yours

Chats aren't sold, shared, or used to target you with ads. You can delete your conversation history anytime.

Age-gated where it matters

Crush mode requires verified age 18+. Yaar is built to be comfortable for a wider audience.

Moderated, not just monitored

Conversations are checked for safety in both directions — what you send and what you receive — before anything is shown.
